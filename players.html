Our Top Players



SOHAIL ABBAS
Top Hockey Player

Sohail Abbas, is a former professional field hockey player from Pakistan who played as a Full-back. Regarded as one of the greatest players of all time he is the all-time second highest goal scorer in international field hockey and highest goal scorer in modern field hockey with 348 international goals.

MAHOOR SHAHZAD
Top Badminton Player

Mahoor Shahzad is a Pakistani badminton player. She has competed at the 2014 Asian Games, and also at the 2018 and 2022 Commonwealth Games.






HARUN HAMID
Top Football Player

Harun Ar-Rashid Faheem Hamid is a professional Pakistani footballer who plays as a midfielder for National League South club St Albans City. Born in England, he plays for the Pakistan national team




MEHAK GUL
Top Chess Player

Mehak Gul started playing chess at the early age of six. She is now 13-year-old and is creating a pro-Pakistan image by being an internationally acclaimed chess player.
